Recipe #1

Description:

A pepperoni pizza recipe uses tomato sauce and oregano on a prebaked crust and is topped with cheese and pepperoni for an easy weeknight dinner!

  • Time: 20 minute total
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Hunt's® Tomato Sauce - 1 can (8 oz each)
grated Parmesan cheese - 1/4 cup
dried oregano - 1/4 teaspoon
prebaked thin pizza crust - 1 (12-inch)

Step 1: Preheat oven to 450°F. Combine tomato sauce, Parmesan cheese and oregano in small bowl; spread tomato sauce evenly over crust. Sprinkle pizza with mozzarella cheese; top with pepperoni.

Step 2:Place on ungreased pizza pan. Bake 12 to 15 minutes or until edges of crust are browned lightly and cheese melts. Cut pizza into 6 slices.

Recipe #2

Description:

A Pot-Sized Pasta recipe with creamy Alfredo sauce, fresh basil and seasoned diced tomatoes added to linguine for an easy one pan recipe

  • Time: 20 minute total
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
reduced-sodium chicken broth - 1 can (14 oz each)
water - 1/2 cup
ddry Pot-Sized(tm) Linguine, uncooked - 8 ounces
light Alfredo pasta sauce - 1 jar (16 oz each)
chopped fresh basil - 1/4 cup
garlic powder - 1/4 teaspoon
ground black pepper - 1/8 teaspoon
Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes with Basil, Garlic and Oregano, drained - 1 can (14.5 oz each)

Step 1: Heat broth and water in large skillet over medium-high heat until simmering. Add linguine. Cover, reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes or until linguine is tender, stirring occasionally.

Step 2: Stir in Alfredo sauce, basil, garlic powder and pepper. Gently stir in drained tomatoes. Cook 2 minutes more or until hot.

Recipe #3

Description:

A quickly prepared Caesar salad dressing recipe made with Egg Beaters for a grilled chicken and romaine lettuce salad!

  • Time: 30 minute total
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
boneless skinless chicken breasts - 1-1/2 pounds
torn romaine lettuce - 12 cups
Egg Beaters® Original - 1/2 cup
extra virgin olive oil - 3 tablespoons
lemon juice - 1/4 cup
clove garlic, minced - 1
Gulden's® Spicy Brown Mustard - 1 tablespoon
salt - 1/4 teaspoon
ground black pepper - 1/4 teaspoon

Step 1: Spray cold grate of outdoor grill with grilling spray. Preheat grill to medium heat according to manufacturer's directions. Place chicken on grate of grill; cover grill with lid. Grill 10 minutes or until no longer pink in centers (165°F), turning after 5 minutes.

Step 2:Cool chicken 10 minutes; cut into strips. Place lettuce in large bowl. Add chicken; mix lightly.

Step 3:Beat Egg Beaters, oil, lemon juice, garlic, mustard, salt and pepper in small bowl with wire whisk until well blended. Pour over salad; toss to coat. Serve immediately.

Source: Ready Set Eat
