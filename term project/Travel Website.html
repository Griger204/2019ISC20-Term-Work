Special Information

Punta Cana, the easternmost tip of the Dominican Republic, abuts the Caribbean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean. It's a region known for its 32km stretch of beaches and clear waters.

Travel guide

When to visit

Punta Cana's climate is warm and tropical year-round. Most travelers arrive Dec–Apr, peaking in late-Dec. Punta Cana's Carnival celebrations (early Mar), the Punta Cana Food & Wine Festival (May/Jun) and the David Ortiz Golf Classic (Dec) bring large crowds to the area. It's hotter and more humid during hurricane season (Jun–Nov).
